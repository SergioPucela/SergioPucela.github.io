Dumb Ways to Get COVID-19

Overview

This game is a collection of mini-games, where the goal is to complete as many as you can before losing 3 times (exposing yourself to the virus 3 times). Each mini-game you win gives you a score and speeds up the game a bit to increase the difficulty.

In this project, I was in charge of the overall mini-games system, but I also ended up designing and programming some of them. The result was a successfully completed project qualified with an excellent grade on didactic games.
