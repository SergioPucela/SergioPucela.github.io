Cards Against Humanity

Overview

Adaptation from an open source multiplayer online card game. It was the first alternative to play the popular board game Cards Against Humanity online in Spanish.

My role in the project consisted entirely in programming cards and translating them. The result was a completely translated and fully playable Cards Against Humanity with 1.000+ users playing every month.
